Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 28, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 28, 2026

AsIf ("we", "our", or "us") is an AI-powered personal styling app that builds outfits from your actual wardrobe. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We've written it to be readable — not legalese.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what's needed to make the app work well for you.

2. Camera & Photos

AsIf requests access to your camera and photo library for the following purposes:

  • Wardrobe scanning — photograph clothing items to add them to your digital closet
  • Selfie analysis — optionally take a selfie so our AI can detect your skin undertone and suggest flattering colours
  • Profile photo — optionally set a profile picture within the app

We do not access your camera or photo library without your explicit action. Photos are never uploaded to our servers without being required for AI processing, and they are not retained after processing is complete.

3. How We Use AI (Google Gemini)

AsIf uses Google's Gemini AI to:

  • Generate daily outfit suggestions from your wardrobe
  • Analyse clothing photos to extract category, colour, and style attributes
  • Process selfie images to detect skin tone and seasonal colour type
  • Answer style questions and generate personalised recommendations
  • Produce trends reports, colour analyses, and capsule wardrobe plans

When content is sent to Gemini for processing, it is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We use Gemini's API under terms that restrict Google from using your data to train their general AI models.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate personalised outfit suggestions tailored to your wardrobe, style, and the weather
  • Build and improve your style profile over time based on your ratings and usage
  • Enforce fair-use limits on AI scan and generation features
  • Process subscription purchases and verify active Pro entitlements
  • Send optional push notifications (outfit reminders, generation completions) if you enable them
  • Enable backup and restore of your wardrobe data

We do not use your information for advertising, sell it to third parties, or use it to profile you for purposes unrelated to the app.

5. Data Storage & Security

The majority of your data — your wardrobe, photos, style preferences, outfit ratings, and usage history — is stored entirely on your device using your device's local storage. It does not leave your device unless you explicitly use the backup/export feature or trigger an AI analysis.

The small amount of data stored in the cloud (your anonymous ID and vision credit balance) is held in Google Firebase, which uses industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit.

Your subscription status is managed by RevenueCat, a dedicated subscription infrastructure provider. We never see or store your payment card details — all payments are processed by the App Store or Google Play.

7. Push Notifications

AsIf can send push notifications for daily outfit reminders and completion alerts (e.g. when a wardrobe scan finishes). These are entirely optional and off by default. You can enable or disable them at any time in the app's Profile tab or in your device's notification settings.

8. Children's Privacy

AsIf is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights & Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete your wardrobe — use "Reset wardrobe" in Profile → Account to wipe all local data instantly
  • Export your data — use "Export backup" in Profile → Backup & restore to download everything
  • Disable notifications — toggle off in Profile → Notifications or in device Settings
  • Revoke camera access — via your device's app permissions settings at any time
  • Cancel subscription — via Google Play subscriptions management
  • Request data deletion — email us and we will delete your anonymous account record from Firebase

10. Data Retention

Local data (wardrobe, style profile, ratings) remains on your device until you reset the app or uninstall it. Cloud data (anonymous ID and vision credit balance) is retained while your account is active. If you request deletion, we will remove your cloud record within 30 days.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify you within the app. Continued use of AsIf after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
